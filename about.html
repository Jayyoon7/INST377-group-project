Purpose

Welcome to our Pokémon Fan Voting website! A platform dedicated to the passionate community of Pokémon enthusiasts. Our mission is to offer fans a unique space where they can express their love for their favorite Pokémon through voting and engaging with fellow fans.

Our Vision

On our voting page you will be shown a random pokemon and you can decide to vote whether you like the pokemon or you're not a fan. You will be shown:

  • An iconic picture of the Pokemon
  • The weight and height of the Pokemon
  • The type of the Pokemon
  • Real time voting results of how many people like and dislike that Pokemon

Our Technology

We are utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the front-end development of our project.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Pokemon?

    • Pokémon is a popular multimedia franchise created by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures, centered around fictional creatures called "Pokémon" which humans, known as Pokémon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport. The franchise includes video games, trading card games, an animated TV series, movies, and various merchandise.

  • Is this website sponsored by Pokemon?

    • No.

  • How do I vote for my favorite Pokemon?

    • While you won't be able to vote for the specific pokemon you are looking for, you can head to our voting page and click on "new Pokemon" button to vote on the random Pokemon that appears. If you're lucky you might find your favorite pokemon you were looking for
