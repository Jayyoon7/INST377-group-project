Purpose

Welcome to the NSFW Detection Project! Our goal is to create a safer digital environment by an application that can automatically identify inappropriate or NSFW in pictures or videos. We aim to provide a reliable solution that ensures a suitable and safe user experience for all, especially children.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a platform that benefits a number of stakeholders:

  • App developers and companies seeking to prioritize user safety and trust.
  • Parents and guardians concerned about their children's online safety.
  • Educators and schools aiming to provide age-appropriate digital content.
  • Content creators ensuring their work is not used inappropriately or misrepresented.
  • Policymakers and regulatory bodies can use data and insights to inform the devlopment of guidelines for creating safer online environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is NSFW content, and why is it important to detect and filer it? NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content is content that is considered inappropriate in certain environemnts, such as workplaces or schools. It is important to detect and filter to ensure the safety and appropriateness of digital environments for user by reducing exposure to material that is explicit or offensive in nature. NSFW detection and filtering is extremely useful for users of younger ages to prevent exposure to potentially harmful material.
